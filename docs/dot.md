# Dot Product

The dot product of two vectors is the sum of the products of the vector components:

$$
\begin{bmatrix}1 \\\2 \end{bmatrix}\dot{}\begin{bmatrix}3 \\\4 \end{bmatrix} \equiv (1 * 3) + (2 * 4) = 14
$$
